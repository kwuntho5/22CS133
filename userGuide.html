User Guide

Introduction

This system aims to provide a reliable and accurate solution for locating the optimal business location using location-based social networks.

To use the Google Map API, you must have a valid API key to replace YOUR_API_KEY in src="https://maps.googleapis.com/maps/api/js?key=YOUR_API_KEY&callback=initMap" in main.html>

User Guide

You may first select the category of your business, which are categorized into 8 groups: Food, Entertainment, Travel, Shop, Nightlife, Sport, Outdoor and Art.

You may also select your preferred candidate market area by entering the number ranged from 001 to 900 in the textbox, each number is a candidate market area in NYC.

Selecting the category of business and candidate market area will filter the table below the map.

Please note that you are not allowed to select candidate market area when you have selected a category of business.

An interactive datatable is shown below the map, you may filter each column or have a global search on the top-right textbox of the table.

The datatable consists of 8 columns, they are Area, Category, Diversity, Competitiveness, Relevacne, Passenger Flow, Overall, Lantitude, Longitude, Map Location

Diversity, Competitiveness, Relevacne, Passenger Flow, Overall are the different factors' and overall popularity values of the calculated results.

You may click map button in Map Location column, the system will direct you to the center of the candidate market area, you may browse the map to look at the nearby environment.
